## Digital Security

| # | Concept | Description |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Pheme Authentication | Centralised login system for all UWA students and staff |
| 2 | Network Segmentation | Separate WiFi networks for students, staff, and guests |
| 3 | DMARC Email Protection | Protects against email spoofing of the UWA domain |
| 4 | Cyber Awareness Training | Mandatory training for all staff |
| 5 | LMS Access Control | Authenticated login required to access course materials |

## Reflection
UWA uses a layered "defence in depth" approach combining physical and digital controls to protect its people and assets.
